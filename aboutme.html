I will never retire

After graduating high school and finishing my track career in 2019, I said, "If I ever run a marathon, I'm never running again!" Throughout college, I lifted weights regularly, but never ran. One morning in 2023, after four years without running, I woke up, went outside, and spontaneously ran for 27 miles. The moment I passed 26.2 miles was one of the greatest highs I've experienced. I remembered that I said I would never run again. But even after finishing...I wanted more, and I realized something. I will never reach a point where I am done. Every achievement is a stepping stone for the next one, and I will always be moving forward and evolving.
